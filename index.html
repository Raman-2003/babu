About me

Hello Vanakkam & welcome!

I am a standup comedian, musician, actor, yoga teacher and an ex(hausted) software engineer, based out of Chennai, India. I did software engineering for fifteen years, while romancing with music and performing arts on the side. I took to standup comedy full time in 2014. My obsession for singing and playing percussion led to me create ‘ALEX IN WONDERLAND’ as a musical standup specia, from August 2019. My second solo ‘ALEXPERIENCE’ which is also a musical standup, has started touring from August, 2022.
Love, Alex
